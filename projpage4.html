How to View Your Accounts

  1. Click "View Accounts".
  2. Enter a number into the box under "Choose the minimum." This will allow you to see which accounts, checking and savings, have at least that balance.
  3. Press "OK."
  4. Select which type of accounts you would like to view, checking or savings, from the drop-down menu. This will allow you to see how many accounts exist of that type.
  5. Press "OK."
  6. The following information will display: all account data, the number of total accounts, accounts that meet the minimum balance that you specified, and the number of accounts that are of the type you specified.

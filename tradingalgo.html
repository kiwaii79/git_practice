Python Trading Algorithm in Quantopian

A simplified trading algorithm that buys securities when their 10-day (short-term) moving-average line crosses their 50-day (long-term) one from below (called a golden cross) and sells all of them when the opposite occurs (called a death cross). The algorithm is written in Python on the electronic trading simulator called Quantopian.

  • Alpha - (+) return in excess of what would be expected for the risk
  • Beta - (+) returns usually have the same sign as the benchmark
  • Sharpe - (+) risk-adjusted performance
This is additional output that is given when my algorithm is backtested.
  • Structural Constraints - measures how many recommended APIs are being used
  • Risk Constraints - measures how well the algo controls certain factors

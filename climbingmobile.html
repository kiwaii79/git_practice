My Mobile Apps

Route Journal

Route Journal allows you to keep track of which climbing gyms/areas you visit and which routes you are working on.

  1. This is the welcome page. Tap "Climbing Website" to see my personal rock climbing training website, tap "Intro" for a brief introduction to the app, or tap "Route Journal" to navigate to your list of climbing areas/gyms.
  2. This is what shows when you tap "Intro."
  3. This is what shows when you tap "Route Journal" from the previous screen. Tap "Edit" to rearrange or delete items off the list or tap "Info" for further instructions. Let's tap on a climbing gym/area name to get to your list of routes for that gym.
  4. You can tap the "+" to add a new route. If you format its name according to the suggestion, then a rock crystal icon will appear next to it that matches the color of the route. Tap "Edit" to rearrange or delete items off the list. Here, you can also tap "Info" for further instructions. Let's tap a route to get to a page where you can take and store a picture of the route.
  5. Tap the Camera icon to take the picture then tap "Save."

BelayChat

BelayChat allows you to coordinate with others so that you can find belay partners that are interested in the same type of climbing as you.

  1. This is the welcome page. Enter your name and approximate climbing level to sign in.
  2. Tap on the type of climbing you would like to find a partner for.
  3. Chat and meet others!
